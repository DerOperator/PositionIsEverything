Bordered Zones Demo

This demo uses red borders to outline the invisible divs that are the true popup elements. The UL elements are just the visible "passengers", and are children of the div popups.

The tiny red boxes are special "mini-zones" used to fill in the corner where users might tend to cut across while moving to the flyout. This mini-zone can be customized to any size and shape, as can the larger hover zones. However, considerable tweaking of margins and widths would be necessary in that case.

Note how the zones of the highest flyout divs cover the top nav, and yet do not prevent direct hovering of those top nav links. Used properly, z-index can do wonderful things!
